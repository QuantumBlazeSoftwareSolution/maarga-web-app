# Maarga: Future Roadmap & Feature Ideas

This document tracks planned features, community suggestions, and experimental ideas for future versions of the Maarga fuel station finder platform.

---

### Phase 1: Enhanced Station Management (Next Up)
- [ ] **Admin Approval Flow**: Implementation of the dashboard to approve/reject `new_station_reports`.
- [ ] **Station Migration**: Automatic migration of approved reports into the main `stations` table.
- [ ] **Trust Score Integration**: Rewarding users with points based on verified data contributions.

### Phase 2: User Experience & Personalization
- [ ] **Favorite Stations**: Allow users to "Star" frequent stations to receive instant push notifications for their specific stock.
- [ ] **Predicted Availability**: AI/ML models based on historical delivery patterns (CPC/LIOC) to predict when a station will run out or get new stock.
- [ ] **Route Optimization**: Instead of just navigating to one station, suggest a multi-stop route if the first choice has long queues.

### Phase 3: Knowledge Base & User Education (NEW)
- [ ] **Fuel Compatibility Hub**: Detailed guide on 92 vs 95 Octane, Super Diesel vs Auto Diesel, and which engine types (Euro 4/5) should use each.
- [ ] **EV Charging Handbook**: Average charging times for common Sri Lankan EVs (Leaf, MG, Hyundai) on Level 1, 2, and DC Fast chargers.
- [ ] **Safety Protocols**: Tips for safe refueling, handling fuel containers, and fire prevention at the shed.
- [ ] **Govt. Quota Status**: Deep links to the National Fuel Pass QR system and quota remaining lookups.

### Phase 4: Community & Social Features
- [ ] **User Comments & Photos**: Allow users to upload photos of the current queue or the "Out of Stock" board at the station.
- [ ] **Verified Contributors**: Visible badges for users with high trust scores to signal reliability to other users.
- [ ] **In-App Messaging**: Brief localized chat for users at the same station to coordinate or share info.

### Phase 5: Hardware & IoT Tracking (NEW)
- [ ] **Maarga Tracker Device**: Custom hardware development of a vehicle tracking module.
    - **Specs**: SIM connectivity (4G/5G), GPS, Built-in Rechargeable + Replaceable dual-battery system, and proprietary onboard charger.
- [ ] **App Integration**: "My Vehicle" live pin in the Maarga map.
- [ ] **Theft Prevention**: Low-battery or geo-fencing exit alerts pushed via the mobile app.

### Phase 6: Beyond Fuel
- [ ] **EV Charging Integration**: Track electric vehicle charging port availability across Sri Lanka.
- [ ] **Service Center Locator**: Find nearby tire repairs, oil changes, or washing centers.
- [ ] **Utility Payments Integration**: Allow users to check fuel quota or QR status directly within the app (if API available).
